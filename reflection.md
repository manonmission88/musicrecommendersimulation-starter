# Reflection Notes

High-Energy Pop versus Chill Lofi: the pop profile pushed songs like Sunrise City and Gym Hero to the top because they match pop and stay energetic, while the lofi profile shifted toward Midnight Coding, Library Rain, and Focus Flow because those songs are slower, softer, and more acoustic. That makes sense because the two profiles are asking for very different moods.

High-Energy Pop versus Deep Intense Rock: both profiles like high energy, but the rock profile preferred Storm Runner because it also matched rock and intense mood, while the pop profile kept Sunrise City first because genre and mood still mattered. This shows that energy alone is not the whole decision.

Chill Lofi versus Deep Intense Rock: these outputs moved in almost opposite directions. Chill lofi favored acoustic and relaxed tracks, while intense rock favored loud, driving songs with low acousticness. That difference is exactly what the preferences were supposed to test.
